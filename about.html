About Impress

Impress Magazine is made with love by teachers in Isesaki. It is a production of the Isesaki Board of Education. The Impress online magazine, Impress smartphone app, and Impress website were developed by Isesaki English Media.

Privacy and Terms of Use

This app doesn't collect any personal information from you. However, we do use analytics tools and cookies on our website, and links to other websites may also collect user data. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of any linked sites. Underlined text links and buttons with the mouse click icon () open new tabs to these external sites. You can read more about our terms of use and our privacy policy at our website: Isesaki.in
